Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about how OWODE works

How long does it take to open an account?

You can open an account in just 5-10 minutes. Once you provide your basic information and upload a valid ID, your account will be active immediately.

What documents do I need?

You need a valid government-issued ID (National ID, Driver's License, or International Passport), proof of income, and an active Nigerian bank account.

How quickly can I get a loan?

Emergency loans can be approved within 2 hours, personal loans within 24 hours, and business loans within 48 hours. Funds are transferred immediately after approval.

Is my money safe with OWODE?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level security measures and all deposits are insured. Your personal and financial information is protected with advanced encryption.

How do I track my savings growth?

You can monitor your savings in real-time through our mobile app or website dashboard. You'll receive regular updates on your interest earnings and account balance.
