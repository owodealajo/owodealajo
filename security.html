Security Best Practices

Help us keep your account secure by following these guidelines

Do's

  • • Use strong, unique passwords for your account
  • • Enable two-factor authentication
  • • Log out completely when using public computers
  • • Keep your contact information updated
  • • Report suspicious activities immediately
  • • Regularly monitor your account statements

Don'ts

  • • Never share your login credentials with anyone
  • • Don't access your account on public Wi-Fi
  • • Never click suspicious links in emails
  • • Don't save passwords in public browsers
  • • Never provide account details over phone calls
  • • Don't ignore security alerts or notifications
