Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your customer service hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday to Friday from 8AM to 6PM, and weekends from 9AM to 4PM. You can also email us anytime for 24/7 support.

How quickly do you respond to emails?

We typically respond to all emails within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our phone support line.

Can I visit your office in person?

Yes! Our Lagos office is open Monday to Friday from 9AM to 5PM. We recommend calling ahead to schedule an appointment for personalized service.

Do you have WhatsApp support?

Yes, you can reach us on WhatsApp at +234 800 123 4567 for quick questions and support. Our WhatsApp support is available during business hours.

What information should I include in my message?

Please include your full name, account number (if applicable), detailed description of your inquiry, and the best way to contact you back.
